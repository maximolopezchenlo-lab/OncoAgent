🚀 Unlocking the Full Power of MI300X! 🚀

1️⃣ Breaking Limits: We just unleashed our Qwen 3.5 9B model on the full 240k+ clinical dataset! 🤯 No more step limits. We are letting the model fully absorb the medical guidelines over 3 epochs. 
2️⃣ Math & Scale: Processing this much data takes time (~62 hours per epoch!), but thanks to the raw power of the AMD MI300X and native ROCm optimization, we're pushing a steady 14-16s per iteration with an effective batch size of 16! ⚡
